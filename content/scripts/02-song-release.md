# I Release What No Longer Chooses Me
`song-release` · song · love · target 3:45

**Suno style prompt:** letting-go ballad, intimate male or androgynous tenor,
fingerpicked acoustic guitar and soft felt piano, subtle strings entering verse 2,
70 BPM, key of A minor resolving to C major in choruses, breath and room left in
