EDITOR'S LETTER— WHY THE MEANING MAY NOT BE YOURS

THE LONGING
MAY BE YOURS.
THE MEANING
MAY NOT.

You learned what it means to succeed. You learned what it means to be loved. You learned what it means to awaken. Nobody asked whether you agreed to the definitions — they were typeset into you early, in somebody else's font.

The longing may be yours. The meaning attached to it may have been inherited. This manual exists to help you tell the two apart, and to choose — deliberately, in your own hand — what gets installed next.
